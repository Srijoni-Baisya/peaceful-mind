A Peaceful Mind


Amidst these times of crisis, when the entire world is fighting the coronavirus pandemic and coping up with the "New Normal", a number of people are facing mental stress. This website is a small effort to help those who are feeling worried throughout the day and thinking of how we can return to normalcy. The website allows you to listen to certaing sooting music that will help you to feel relaxed. You can even play them in the background while doing meditation. Besides, it provides a link to check good news regarding COVID-19, instead of negative thoughts. Meditation is a great way to overcome stress and feel refreshed. Apart from this, breathing exercises are also an important precaution for the coronavirus disease. This website helps you to learn a few common breathing exercises step-by-step, which you can practise regularly at home.
